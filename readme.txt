hipKartMobi is a powerful plugin that helps you make WooCommerce Store run on your own branded native Mobile Apps. Seamlessly.

== Description ==

hipKartMobi is a free plugin that allows you to sell anything with WooCommerce on your branded Mobile App. Built to integrate seamlessly with WooCommerce, hipKartMobi is the wonderful solution that gives store owners to reach Mobile Customers.

= Manage Category and Products =
With hipKartMobi, you can link your woocommerce categories to hipKart's Categories, Also Products can be added or update to your Mobile App automatically as you add or update them. You can push any numbers of products to your hipkart store which will be listed on your Store Mobile App.


= Inventory and Shipment =
With hipKartMobi, Once you link your hipKart store, every time user Add Product into Cart from your Store App this will check for available stock and during checkout App get information about shipping charges from your woocommerce store, So basically you don't have to manage Inventory or Shipping separately on hipKart store, it will be managed automatically.
 
= Currency and Payment options =
With hipKartMobi, your WooCommerce store currency will be used on your Mobile App. 
For Payment you need to setup your Payment Gateway into hipKart store admin which is a few clicks thing. hipkart support all major payment gateways like PayPal, PayKloud, Stripe, Authorize.Net, LiqPay and Amazon Payments.


= You control it all -- forever =
hipKart gives you complete control, your store is always free. You can manage this as per your needs and all Payments will always directly reaches into your merchant account & bank account. 

= Orders Synchronization =
With hipKartMobi, Once you link your hipKart store, every time an order is placed, will get added into your WooCommerce store orders list, so that you can process your Order from WooCommerce Store, you don't need to manage orders in hipKart Store although most store owners like to use hipkartâ€™s free mobile app to manage their orders on the go.

= No need of development Skills =
With hipKartMobi, you only need to agree to link your WooCommerce store with your new free hipKart Store and rest will be managed automatically, the plugin have settings if want to disable it any time. (Fact : No one disables it, as everyone likes things to work seamlessly & effortless.)

= Your hipKartMobi Store App =
[hipKart.Mobi](https://www.hipkart.mobi/) is a platform to get Android and iOS native Mobile Apps for your online Store. You can open this link and can choose which App you want for your store Android or iOS or Both. [hipKart.Mobi](https://www.hipkart.mobi/) is powered by [hipKart](https://www.hipkart.com/sell/), which is a free platform to build online store. 

Once your create a free store and get your App, you only need to open hipKartMobi plugin page, login with your username and password of hipKart store, which you can get free from [hipKart](https://www.hipkart.com/sell/) or [hipKart.Mobi](https://www.hipkart.mobi/) and process will start and your App will be synced with your new orders as well hipkart will send you new orders from its discovery network.

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* [WooCommerce Plugin](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/)
* [hipKart.Mobi](https://www.hipkart.mobi/) Mobile App 


= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of WooCommerce, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type "hipKartMobi" and click Search Plugins. Once you've found our plugin you can view details about it such as the point release, rating and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by simply clicking "Install Now".

= Manual installation =

The manual installation method involves downloading our hipKartMobi plugin and uploading it to your web server via your favourite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](https://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation).

= Updating =

Automatic updates should work like a charm; as always though, ensure you backup your site just in case.
